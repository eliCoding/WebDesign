Where East Meets West

Tozai is Japanese for “East-West.” It epitomizes the welcome we offer our guests, combining western-style luxury with the best in Japanese cuisine and hospitality.

Escape from the bustle and stress of everyday life, and relax in the comfort and tranquility of Tozai Hotel’s guest rooms and gardens. Our expert chefs are ready to delight your taste buds with sashimi and sushi, made only from the choicest cuts of fish delivered daily from Tsukiji market in Tokyo.

Make Tozai Hotel your home away from home.
