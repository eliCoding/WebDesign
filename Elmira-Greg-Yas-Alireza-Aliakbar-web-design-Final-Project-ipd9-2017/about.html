What is Melody Art Music Center?

Melody Art Music Center is a privately owned and operated music school offering personalized private music lessons, rock band classes, music for toddlers programs, recitals and recordings. Known for their highly qualified teachers, quality lesson sessions and friendly, welcoming environment; Melody Art Music Center has made a name for itself in the private music education sector. We also offer a rock band program in our Rock ‘N’ Roll Academy and a Music for Toddler program as well. For your convenience, we also stock instrument accessories and music books.

About Us

Our Goals

Our goal at Melody Art Music Center is to set a precedent in the privately taught music education industry. Our focus is to develop a unique and exciting musical learning environment for students of all ages, ranging from beginner to advanced levels. We aim to provide unique, individualized musical education by offering new and innovative curriculum tailored to personal learning approaches, pace of learning and preferences of our students. It is our belief that students should have choices and options when it comes to their musical education. Here at Melody Art Music Center, we strive to help our students achieve THEIR goals, rather than the expectations of others in a comfortable, friendly atmosphere.

Qualified Teachers

Melody Art Music Center Teachers are professional musicians holding Bachelor, Master or Doctorate degrees in music or their equivalents as defined by the Canada Council for the Arts. In addition to meeting our high professional standards, Melody Art Music Center Teachers must also undergo a full, selective interview process. Our teachers are University professors and lecturers as well as young, dynamic, professional musicians. We maintain our standards of excellence through ongoing feedback and support, as well as annual teacher reviews. With these processes in place, we strive to maintain the highest possible standards available in music schools today.
